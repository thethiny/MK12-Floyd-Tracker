Privacy Policy

Data Handling

Your data is never stored; it is forwarded directly to Warner Bros and MK APIs.

Only publicly available profiles can be accessed using this tool.

Anyone with your generated Floyd Tracker URL can view your stats. Please share responsibly.

The Tracker does not store your information, everything is recalculated each time you visit the website.

Cookies & Local Storage

Floyd Tracker uses your browser's local storage to save previously tracked usernames. This helps you search faster without retyping the same usernames.

No data is sent to any server when storing usernames locally. Everything stays on your device. You can clear this saved history anytime from the website.
